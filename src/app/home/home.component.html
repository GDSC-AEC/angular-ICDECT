About

What is it about?

The main aim of the conference is to offer a real opportunity to bring together researchers of different disciplines, to discuss and share new issues, to tackle complex problems and to find advanced solutions breeding trends in Computer Science, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering. Conference will also provide a platform for academicians, research community and industry to share the recent developments, and to discuss about the discoveries in computational intelligence and informatics. 6th International Conference on Data Engineering and Communication Technology (ICDECT-2022) is scheduled to be held on August 12th & 13th, 2022 and is organized by Department of CSE, Aditya Engineering College(A), Aditya Nagar, ADB Road, Surampalem -533437 from August 12th & 13th, 2022 in association with Springer.
